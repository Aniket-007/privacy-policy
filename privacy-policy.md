# Privacy Policy for The Notes App

Effective Date: 16 January 2026

The Notes App ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use The Notes App.

---

## Information We Collect

The Notes App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data to any external servers.

All data created by the user is stored locally on the user's device only.

### Data stored locally includes:
- Note titles and descriptions
- Reminder date and time
- Pin status of notes

---

## Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions only to provide core functionality:

- POST_NOTIFICATIONS  
Used to display reminder notifications set by the user.

- SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM  
Used to trigger reminders and alarms at the exact time chosen by the user.

These permissions are not used to collect or share personal information.

---

## Internet Usage

The Notes App does not require an internet connection.

- No data is uploaded to servers
- No cloud storage is used
- No third-party services or analytics are integrated

---

## Data Sharing

We do not share user data with any third party.

No advertisements, tracking, or analytics SDKs are used in this app.

---

## Data Security

All user data remains on the user's device.

We do not have access to your notes or reminders.

Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

---

## Children's Privacy

The Notes App is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
